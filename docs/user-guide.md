# EV Charging Slot Booking System - User Guide

## Welcome!

This guide will help you use the EV Charging Slot Booking System to find and reserve charging slots for your electric vehicle.

## What is this system?

The EV Charging Slot Booking System is a simple web application that helps you:
- Find nearby electric vehicle charging stations
- Check which charging slots are available
- Book a charging slot in advance
- Manage your bookings
- Add multiple vehicles to your account

## Getting Started

### System Requirements

You need:
- A computer or mobile device with internet access
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Your location (to find nearby stations)

### Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser
2. Go to the system URL (provided by your administrator)
3. You'll see the login page

## Using the System

### 1. Creating an Account

**First-time users need to register:**

1. Click the "Register" button on the login page
2. Fill in your information:
   - **Username**: Choose a unique username (3+ characters)
   - **Email**: Your email address
   - **Password**: Create a secure password (6+ characters)
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password
3. Click "Register"
4. You'll be automatically logged in and taken to your dashboard

### 2. Logging In

**If you already have an account:**

1. Enter your username and password
2. Click "Login"
3. You'll be taken to your dashboard

### 3. Your Dashboard

The dashboard is your main page where you can:
- See an overview of your account
- Quick access to main features
- View your recent bookings
- See how many vehicles you have registered

**From the dashboard, you can:**
- Browse charging stations
- Add new vehicles
- View all your bookings

### 4. Adding Your Vehicles

Before booking, you need to add your vehicles:

1. Click "Browse Stations" from your dashboard
2. The system will prompt you to add a vehicle if you don't have any
3. Fill in vehicle details:
   - **Vehicle Type**: Choose "2-wheeler" or "4-wheeler"
   - **License Plate**: Enter your vehicle's license plate number
4. Click "Add Vehicle"

You can add multiple vehicles if you have more than one electric vehicle.

### 5. Finding Charging Stations

**To find charging stations near you:**

1. Click "Stations" in the top menu
2. The system will try to get your current location automatically
3. If location access is denied, you can:
   - Enter your latitude and longitude coordinates manually
   - Or use a known location's coordinates
4. Choose your search radius (5km, 10km, 20km, or 50km)
5. Click "Use Current Location" or wait for automatic search

**Understanding station results:**
- Each station shows the name, address, and distance from you
- The number of available slots is displayed
- Click on any station to see available slots and make a booking

### 6. Booking a Charging Slot

**Step-by-step booking process:**

1. **Select a Station**: From the stations list, click on a station
2. **Choose Your Vehicle**: Select which vehicle you want to charge
3. **Select Date and Time**: 
   - Choose the date for your charging session
   - Set start time and end time
   - Minimum booking is 1 hour
4. **Available Slots**: The system shows which slots are free
   - Green slots are available
   - Each slot shows the number and type (2-wheeler/4-wheeler)
   - Click on an available slot to select it
5. **Confirm Booking**: Click "Confirm Booking" to reserve the slot

**Important booking rules:**
- You can only book slots that match your vehicle type
- Bookings must be for future times (not in the past)
- Once booked, a slot cannot be booked by others for that time period
- You can cancel bookings up until the start time

### 7. Managing Your Bookings

**To view your bookings:**

1. Click "My Bookings" in the top menu
2. You'll see all your bookings (active, cancelled, and completed)

**Booking information includes:**
- Station name and address
- Slot number and type
- Vehicle details
- Date and time
- Booking status (active, cancelled, completed)

**Cancelling a booking:**
1. Find the booking you want to cancel
2. Click "Cancel Booking" (only available for active future bookings)
3. Confirm the cancellation
4. The slot becomes available for others

### 8. Admin Features (For Administrators Only)

If you have admin access, you can:

**Manage Stations:**
- Add new charging stations
- View all stations in the system
- Edit station information

**View All Bookings:**
- See bookings from all users
- Monitor system usage
- View booking statistics

## Tips for Best Experience

### Finding Stations
- Enable location services in your browser for automatic location detection
- Use larger search radius if you don't find nearby stations
- Check station addresses to ensure they're convenient for you

### Booking Slots
- Book during off-peak hours for better availability
- Cancel bookings you don't need to free up slots for others
- Book in advance during busy periods

### Vehicle Management
- Keep your license plate information accurate
- Add all your electric vehicles for convenience
- Remove vehicles you no longer own

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**"Can't find my location"**
- Enable location services in your browser settings
- Try refreshing the page and allowing location access
- Enter coordinates manually if location services don't work

**"No slots available"**
- Try different time slots
- Look for stations further away
- Try booking for a different date

**"Login failed"**
- Check your username and password
- Make sure Caps Lock is off
- Click "Register" if you don't have an account

**"Booking failed"**
- Ensure all required fields are filled
- Check that your end time is after start time
- Make sure the booking time is in the future
- Try refreshing and trying again

### Getting Help

If you need assistance:
1. Check this guide first
2. Try refreshing the page
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
4. Contact your system administrator

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I book the same slot multiple times?**
A: Yes, but for different time periods. Each booking is for a specific time slot.

**Q: What if I'm late for my booking?**
A: Your slot is reserved for the entire booked period. If you're very late, you may lose part of your charging time.

**Q: Can I change my booking time?**
A: Currently, you need to cancel the existing booking and create a new one.

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**
A: You can book any future time. There's no upper limit on advance booking.

**Q: Is there a cost for booking?**
A: This system is for slot reservation only. Payment for charging would be handled separately at the station.

**Q: What if the station is full when I arrive?**
A: Your booked slot is guaranteed. Show your booking details to station staff if needed.
